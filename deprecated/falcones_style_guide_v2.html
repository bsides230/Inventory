The primary typeface for readability is Inter. It is used for app content, product listings, operational notes, ordering screens, and descriptive text.

Playfair Display adds warmth and a subtle bistro feel for headings and branded moments, while JetBrains Mono is reserved for labels, data, IDs, and structured interface metadata.
